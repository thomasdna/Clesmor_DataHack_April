# Expansion Copilot — Demo Story (90 seconds)

## Goal (what judges should remember)
**Expansion Copilot turns messy “where should we expand?” debates into an explainable, map-based shortlist for Sydney gyms.**

## 90-second live demo flow (screen order)

### Screen 1 — “Question + controls” (10s)
**Narrative**: “I’m a gym growth lead. I want one answer: *where in Sydney should we open next, and why?*”

### Screen 2 — “Ranked shortlist” (20s)
Show the ranked table of candidate areas.
**Narrative**: “Here are the top areas, ranked by opportunity — not just density — with the score broken into competitors, complements, and data quality.”

### Screen 3 — “Map + click a winner” (20s)
Click the #1 area on the map/table.
**Narrative**: “Let’s open the explanation panel for the top candidate.”

### Screen 4 — “Why here?” explanation (25s)
Show:
- competitor count + examples
- complement mix (transit/grocery/offices etc.)
- data quality score note
**Narrative**: “This area wins because it has strong complement activity, manageable competition, and solid coverage — the model can justify the recommendation.”

### Screen 5 — “Compare 2–3 areas” (10s)
Select 2–3 candidates and show side-by-side.
**Narrative**: “Now we can compare finalists in seconds and export for stakeholders.”

### Screen 6 — “Export” (5s)
Export CSV.
**Narrative**: “One click: a shortlist with the rationale, ready to send.”

## Strong closing line
**“Expansion Copilot doesn’t just say *where* — it explains *why*, so expansion decisions become faster and defensible.”**
